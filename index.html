Dev Community Building

As a group of committed and youthful developers, we are in charge of the university's technology club. We work in collaboration with local development groups to create developer conferences and meetups in their respective areas of expertise.

Learning Through Collaboration

We are keen to learn about new technology that we can include into our arsenal. Collaboration is our primary method for learning new technologies quickly and delivering what the institution requires.

Our Specialty

Here are the tools and technologies that we use.

Data Structures & Algorithms

Our student developers are fluent in writing high performant and efficient programs. This is due to their extensive knowledge in implementing advance Data Structures and Algorithms

Software Integration

We provide APIs and User Interfaces so that students may readily access the information. This is accomplished through the usage of a variety of backend technologies, including Node JS, Django, and Firebase.

Mobile Development

In Cebu Technological University, mobile phones are used as the primary learning tool by the vast majority of students enrolled there. In order to provide a better experience, we create apps using Java and Kotlin

Complex Web Services

We use AWS EC2, S3 and CloudFront for our server infrastracture. This allows us to build complex softwares that automatically scales to serve more students in accessing our softwares. There won't be issue with the use of this tech.
